About

The resort is the right choice for visitors who are searching for a combination of charm, peace and quiet, and a convenient position. It is a small, comfortable resort. The staff offer an attentive, personalized service and are always available to offer any help to guests. Two guest houses are arranged on the ground floor. Apart from the reception, there is a comfortable lounge where you can sit and drink tea, or just read. There is also a splendid terrace, where, you can relax and immerge yourself from morning onwards in the good atmosphere, travelling about by water and people gathering together and filling the alleyways and little squares with their chatter.

The resort provides an internet point, and a Wi-Fi service.
